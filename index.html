We are the Monkees

About The Band

With over 50 years experience, and several number one hits; the Monkee's are truly honoured with the career they have been given.

The 4 boys (Davy Jones, Micky Dolenz, Michael Nesmith & Peter Tork) have hit the heights of any rock band from the 60's in the USA. Many hits such as "I'm a Believer" and "Last Train to Clarksville" touched the hearts of millions worldwide!

After retirement in the touring world, the band would like to announce the availability of performing at events such as weddings and Christmas parties.
